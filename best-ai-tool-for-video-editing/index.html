What is the best free AI video editor?

CapCut and DaVinci Resolve offer robust free tiers with AI features like auto-captions and background removal.

Can AI edit videos automatically?

Yes. Tools like Pictory and OpusClip use AI to detect highlights, remove silences, and reframe videos for social media.

Is AI video editing worth it for beginners?

Absolutely. AI reduces the learning curve by automating complex tasks like color grading, audio sync, and captioning.
